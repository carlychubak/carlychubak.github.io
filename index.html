Carly Chubak Fine Art

Carly Chubak was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. She has a degree in Physics & Astrophysics (with Honors) from UC Berkeley and is now studying Fine Art at Art Center College of Design. She is currently focused on soft sculpture and textile/fiber installations. Her experience in scientific data analysis informs her art in subtle ways, including an emphasis on the minutiae and on repetitive work, particularly in her use of stitching.

Please view pieces via the menu above. Works in progress can often be found on Instagram under @whatcarlysees. Please direct any and all inquiries to carlychubak at gmail dot com.
