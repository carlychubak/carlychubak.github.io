Carly Chubak

Carly Chubak was born and raised in Los Feliz. She has a degree in Physics & Astrophysics (with Honors) from UC Berkeley and is currently studying Fine Art at Art Center College of Design. She is currently focused on soft sculpture and textile/fiber installations. Her experience in scientific data analysis informs her art in subtle ways, including an emphasis on the minutiae and on repetitive work, particularly in her use of stitching.
